Our Story

Welcome to Plushie Paradise! We start our journey in a small home studio. We had only one simple goal: to make the best friends for everyone. For us, a plushie is not only a toy. It is a friend who can keep all your secrets and give you comfort.

Every toy in our shop is handmade. We use materials that is safe for children and adults too. We really love our work. We try to make every toy unique, so every plushie have it's own personality.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: we want to make people smile. We sell high-quality and eco-friendly plush toys. Our dream is to be the best toy store in our city and help local workers.
